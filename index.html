Why Choose a Teacup Puppy?

  • Perfect for apartment living and small spaces
  • Easy to travel with and take on adventures
  • Lower food and supply costs compared to larger breeds
  • Ideal companions for seniors or those with limited mobility
  • Endless cuteness in a tiny package!
