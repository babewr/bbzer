Fun Facts

Oranges were historically so expensive that they were given as Christmas gifts!

The name orange comes from the Sanskrit word for "orange tree" - naranga.

Brazil produces more oranges than any other country in the world.

Orange trees can live for over 100 years and still produce fruit!

Dried orange slices can last for months and are perfect for decorations and tea!

It takes about 3-4 medium oranges to make one cup of fresh orange juice.

The first automatic citrus juicer was invented in 1932 by Norman Walker!
