What is a CatDog?

A CatDog is a marvelous creature that combines the best of both feline and canine worlds. Imagine the purr-fect cuddle buddy with the loyalty of a dog and the independence of a cat!

CatDog Characteristics

  • Meows and barks interchangeably
  • Chases its own tail... and catches it!
  • Enjoys both catnip and dog treats
  • Can use a litterbox or go for walks
  • Expert at fetching toys... but might not bring them back

How to Care for Your CatDog

Caring for a CatDog is an adventure! They need a scratching post next to their doghouse, and enjoy playing with balls of yarn and frisbees equally. Feed them a diet of premium cat food mixed with dog kibble for optimal health.

Famous CatDogs in Pop Culture

While CatDog from the Nickelodeon show might be the most famous, our real-life CatDogs are making waves in the pet world. Keep an eye out for them in upcoming pet shows and adorable animal videos!
